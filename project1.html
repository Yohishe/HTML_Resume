KnightForecast

Website intended to be used by UCF students and faculty to inform of upcoming weather and campus events

The prototype depicts the flow of the app. Users could log in, view upcoming events, see related weather and traffic details, as well as use a map feature to navigate to classsrooms and see the temperature of the room. This was done as a group project with multiple stages of prototyping and user testing.
